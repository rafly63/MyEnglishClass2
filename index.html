About Me

Get to know your dedicated English educator

Assalamualaikum Warahmatullahi Wabarakatuh

Praise be to Allah SWT for all the grace and blessings, so that I can complete the creation of this learning website. Let me introduce myself, I am Yetno Hartono, S.Pd., an English teacher at SMAN 1 Sumenep. I was born in Sumenep on September 26, 1985.
Segala puji bagi Allah SWT atas segala rahmat dan karunia-Nya, sehingga saya dapat menyelesaikan pembuatan situs web pembelajaran ini. Perkenalkan, saya Yetno Hartono, S.Pd., seorang guru Bahasa Inggris di SMAN 1 Sumenep. Saya lahir di Sumenep pada tanggal 26 September 1985.

As an educator, I feel very fortunate to be able to share knowledge and experience with students. Besides teaching English, I also have hobbies in adventure, such as traveling and mountain climbing. These activities not only refresh the mind but also provide new inspiration in the learning process.
Sebagai seorang pendidik, saya merasa sangat beruntung dapat berbagi ilmu dan pengalaman dengan para siswa. Selain mengajar bahasa Inggris, saya juga memiliki hobi petualangan, seperti traveling dan mendaki gunung. Kegiatan-kegiatan ini tidak hanya menyegarkan pikiran tetapi juga memberikan inspirasi baru dalam proses pembelajaran.

Before teaching at SMAN 1 Sumenep, I used to teach Mandarin/Chinese in several schools in Malang, such as SMKN 1 Turen, SMKN 1 Pujon, SMAN 3 Malang, and SMPN 5 Malang. This experience of teaching various languages gave me a broader understanding of the language learning process in general.
Sebelum mengajar di SMAN 1 Sumenep, saya pernah mengajar bahasa Mandarin/Tionghoa di beberapa sekolah di Malang, seperti SMKN 1 Turen, SMKN 1 Pujon, SMAN 3 Malang, dan SMPN 5 Malang. Pengalaman mengajar berbagai bahasa ini memberi saya pemahaman yang lebih luas tentang proses pembelajaran bahasa secara umum.

Since 2009 until now, I have dedicated myself to SMAN 1 Sumenep. Through this website, I want to create a more interactive and enjoyable learning space for students. Here, students can find various learning materials, practice questions, and the latest information about English.
Sejak tahun 2009 hingga sekarang, saya telah mendedikasikan diri untuk SMAN 1 Sumenep. Melalui website ini, saya ingin menciptakan ruang belajar yang lebih interaktif dan menyenangkan bagi para siswa. Di sini, siswa dapat menemukan berbagai materi pembelajaran, soal latihan, dan informasi terbaru seputar bahasa Inggris.

Hopefully, this website can be beneficial for everyone, students, teachers, and the wider community. Constructive criticism and suggestions are highly expected so that this website can continue to grow and become a quality learning resource.
Semoga situs web ini bermanfaat bagi semua orang, baik siswa, guru, maupun masyarakat luas. Kritik dan saran yang membangun sangat kami harapkan agar situs web ini dapat terus berkembang dan menjadi sumber belajar yang berkualitas.

Wassalamualaikum Warahmatullahi Wabarakatuh

Learning Objectives

For the 1st semester of Academic Year 2025-2026

For the 2025-2026 academic year (1st semester), the learning objectives for English in Phase E is:
Students are able to understand, identify the purpose, text structure, and linguistic features, design, compose and present : Descriptive text and Recount text"

Today's Learning Objectives are :

  1. 1st Meeting
    • Asesmen Diagnostik Non-kognitif
  2. 2nd Meeting
    • Learning Video 1 (Full English)
    • Learning Video 2 (Billingual)
    • Summary of the Lesson : Direct - Indirect Speech (Quoted - Reported Speech)
    • Exercises: Latihan Soal
    • Assessment 2: Direct - Indirect Speech
  3. 3rd Meeting
    • Summary of the Lesson : Preposition of Time
    • Exercises: Latihan Soal
    • Assessment 3: Preposition of Time
  4. 4th Meeting
    • Summary of the Lesson : Passive Sentence
    • Exercises: Latihan Soal
    • Assessment 4: Passive Sentence
